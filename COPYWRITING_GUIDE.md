# ZaZen Website - Copywriting Style Guide
## Voice, Tone, and Messaging Reference

---

## 1. BRAND VOICE

### Core Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Confident** | Bold statements without hedging | "40% energy savings" not "up to 40%" |
| **Technical** | Precise specifications | "±0.5°C" not "very precise" |
| **Visionary** | Forward-looking | "Built for India's reality. Engineered for the future." |
| **Trustworthy** | Backed by evidence | "10-year compressor warranty" |
| **Indian** | Local context, global standards | "Make in India" + "International compliance" |

### Voice Don'ts
- ❌ Don't use hype words: "revolutionary," "game-changing," "disruptive"
- ❌ Don't use vague claims: "very efficient," "much better," "significant savings"
- ❌ Don't talk down to technical buyers
- ❌ Don't use generic SaaS language: "streamline," "optimize," "leverage"
- ❌ Don't overpromise: "100% guaranteed" (unless legally true)

### Voice Do's
- ✅ Use specific numbers and data
- ✅ Use active voice
- ✅ Lead with customer benefit
- ✅ Use industry terminology correctly
- ✅ Show, don't tell (data over adjectives)

## 2. TONE BY SECTION

### Hero: Inspirational + Authoritative
- Establish category leadership
- Create aspiration
- Communicate premium positioning

**Tone markers:**
- Short, punchy statements
- Strong verbs
- Command headlines
- Minimal punctuation

**Example:**
```
INTELLIGENT COLD CHAIN
FOR MODERN INDIA

40% energy savings. Zero temperature variance.
Solar-ready from day one.
```

### Problem: Empathetic + Data-Driven
- Acknowledge pain without being negative
- Use data to validate emotions
- Create urgency through facts

**Tone markers:**
- Direct statements about losses
- Counter format (BIG NUMBER + context)
- Industry-standard comparisons

**Example:**
```
INDIA'S COLD CHAIN IS BROKEN

40% FOOD WASTE RATE
India loses 40% of perishable food to temperature failures

±3°C VARIANCE INDUSTRY STANDARD
Traditional freezers swing wildly, destroying product quality
```

### Solution: Technical + Confident
- Introduce technology with clarity
- Show superiority through specs
- Build credibility with details

**Tone markers:**
- Component names (proper capitalization)
- Specification callouts
- Benefit linking (spec → outcome)

**Example:**
```
BLDC VARIABLE-SPEED COMPRESSOR
Adaptive 2,200-4,200 RPM control delivers 40% energy reduction.
No fixed-speed inefficiency. No temperature swings.
```

### Products: Clear + Conversion-Focused
- Easy to understand options
- Clear pricing signals
- Direct CTAs

**Tone markers:**
- Tabular information
- Price anchoring
- Size/capacity clarity

**Example:**
```
ZF-Series Smart Freezers
Complete intelligent units from ₹85,000
200L to 1500L capacity range
```

### Comparison: Factual + Convincing
- Side-by-side superiority
- Quantified advantages
- Visual data presentation

**Tone markers:**
- Table format
- Green highlighting for wins
- Percentage differences

**Example:**
```
Traditional: 4.8 kWh/day
ZaZen: 2.9 kWh/day (-40%)
```

### Industries: Specialized + Relatable
- Speak the industry's language
- Address specific pain points
- Show sector expertise

**Tone markers:**
- Industry terminology
- Scenario-specific examples
- ROI calculations per sector

**Example:**
```
ICE CREAM RETAIL
Crystal-clear texture. Zero crystal formation. Perfect scoop every time.

THE CHALLENGE
Ice cream suffers from temperature abuse. Every ±1°C fluctuation
creates ice crystals, destroys texture, and ruins the customer experience.
```

### Trust: Evidence-Based + Reassuring
- Proof over promises
- Third-party validation
- Warranty as confidence

**Tone markers:**
- Certification names
- Warranty periods
- Quote attribution

**Example:**
```
10-YEAR COMPRESSOR WARRANTY
Not a typo. A statement of confidence.

"The 10-year warranty tells you everything about their confidence."
— Equipment Manager, Leading Ice Cream Chain
```

### CTA: Urgent + Low-Friction
- Clear next step
- Multiple options
- Risk removal

**Tone markers:**
- Action verbs
- "Free" / "No obligation"
- Alternative contact methods

**Example:**
```
READY TO TRANSFORM YOUR COLD CHAIN?

See the difference in your own facility.
Schedule a no-obligation demonstration.

[Request Free Demo]
```

## 3. HEADLINE FORMULAS

### Formula 1: Category + Modifier
```
INTELLIGENT COLD CHAIN FOR MODERN INDIA
[Category] for [Target Market]

Examples:
- Smart Refrigeration for Growing Businesses
- Solar-Ready Freezers for Rural India
```

### Formula 2: Number + Benefit
```
40% ENERGY SAVINGS. ZERO TEMPERATURE VARIANCE.
[Quantified Benefit]. [Absolute Benefit].

Examples:
- 10-Year Warranty. Zero Maintenance Worries.
- 99.8% Lower Emissions. 100% Performance.
```

### Formula 3: Problem → Solution
```
INDIA'S COLD CHAIN IS BROKEN
MEET THE INTELLIGENT ALTERNATIVE

[Problem Statement]
[Solution Introduction]

Examples:
- Unreliable Grid. Reliable Refrigeration.
- Rising Power Costs. Falling Energy Bills.
```

### Formula 4: Technical Spec → Outcome
```
BLDC VARIABLE-SPEED TECHNOLOGY
Adaptive control for consistent temperature

[Technology Name]
[Customer Outcome]

Examples:
- 9-Sensor IoT Array. Complete Visibility.
- R290 Natural Refrigerant. 99.8% Cleaner.
```

### Formula 5: Imperative + Result
```
CHOOSE YOUR SOLUTION
Find the perfect fit for your operation

[Action]
[Benefit/Reward]

Examples:
- Calculate Your Savings. See Your ROI.
- Request a Demo. Experience the Difference.
```

## 5. SPECIFIC PHRASES & TERMINOLOGY

### Technical Terms (Always Use Correctly)
- BLDC (Brushless DC) - not "inverter"
- EEV (Electronic Expansion Valve) - not "expansion valve"
- GWP (Global Warming Potential) - always with number
- kWh/day - not "units per day"
- ±0.5°C - not "plus minus 0.5 degrees"
- R290 - proper refrigerant naming

### Brand Terms (Always Capitalize)
- ZF-Series
- ZaZen Cloud
- ZaZen Systems (company name)
- BLDC (acronym)
- IoT (acronym)
- Make in India (when referencing the initiative)

### Avoid (Use Instead)
- "Inverter compressor" → "BLDC variable-speed compressor"
- "Very efficient" → "40% energy savings"
- "Green refrigerant" → "R290 natural refrigerant"
- "Smart freezer" → "Intelligent freezer" or "IoT-enabled"
- "Cheap" → "Lower total cost of ownership"
- "Best" → "India's only" / "validated 40% savings"

## 9. ERROR MESSAGES

### Form Validation
```
Required field: "Please enter your [field name]"
Invalid email: "Please enter a valid email address"
Invalid phone: "Please enter a valid 10-digit mobile number"
Server error: "Something went wrong. Please try again or call us at +91 XXXXX XXXXX."
```

### 404 Page
```
Headline: Page Not Found
Body: The page you're looking for doesn't exist or has been moved.
CTA: "Return to Homepage" | "View Products" | "Contact Support"
```

## 10. LOCALIZATION NOTES

### Indian English Preferences
- Use "lakh" not "hundred thousand" (₹7.2L not ₹720,000)
- Use "crore" for large numbers when appropriate
- Use Indian date format: DD-MM-YYYY
- Use IST for time zone references
- Include Hindi transliterations for key terms if needed:
  - ठंडा श्रृंखला (Cold Chain)
  - बिजली बचत (Power Savings)

### Number Formatting
- Currency: ₹85,000 (Indian numbering system)
- Decimals: 2.9 kWh (dot for decimal)
- Percentages: 40% (no space)
- Temperature: -18°C to -22°C (en-dash for range)

## 11. A/B TEST IDEAS

### Headline Tests
| Variant A | Variant B | Hypothesis |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| "Intelligent Cold Chain for Modern India" | "40% Energy Savings. Zero Temperature Variance." | Spec-first may convert technical buyers faster |
| "Request Demo" | "See It In Action" | Action-oriented language may increase CTR |
| "₹7.2L Saved Over 10 Years" | "Save ₹60,000 Every Year" | Annual framing may feel more tangible |

### CTA Tests
| Variant A | Variant B | Hypothesis |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| "Request Free Demo" | "Book Your Free Demo" | "Book" implies scheduling, may increase commitment |
| "Calculate Your Savings" | "See Your 10-Year ROI" | Specific timeframe may increase relevance |

### Social Proof Tests
| Variant A | Variant B | Hypothesis |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| "Trusted by Industry Leaders" | Logos only | Logos alone may reduce cognitive load |
| "10-Year Warranty" | "10-Year Compressor Warranty" | Specificity may increase trust |

## 12. COPY REVIEW CHECKLIST

Before publishing any copy, verify:

- [ ] No hype words (revolutionary, game-changing, disruptive)
- [ ] All numbers are specific and validated
- [ ] Technical terms are used correctly
- [ ] Brand names are capitalized properly
- [ ] Currency uses Indian format (₹XX,XXX)
- [ ] Temperature notation is correct (±0.5°C)
- [ ] Headlines use command or statement form
- [ ] CTAs are action-oriented
- [ ] Proof points follow claims
- [ ] Industry sections use appropriate terminology
- [ ] Voice is confident, not arrogant
- [ ] Tone matches the section purpose
- [ ] Copy is scannable (short paragraphs, bullet points)
- [ ] Mobile version reads well (shorter lines)
